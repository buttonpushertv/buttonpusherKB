
=========================== GOOGLE-CHROME-HOTKEYS ===========================









============================== YOUTUBE-HOTKEYS ==============================

SPACEBAR or K <-- Play/Pause Video

J <-- Seek backwards 10 seconds
L <-- Seek forwards 10 seconds

Left <-- With seek bar focused, skip backwards 5 seconds
Right <-- With seek bar focused, skip forwards 5 seconds

< <-- Reduce playback speed
> <-- Increase playback speed

Home <-- Seek to start of the video
End <-- Seek to the last few seconds of the video

Number 0 <-- Seek to start of the video

Numbers 1-9 <-- Seek to the 10% to 90% of the video

m <-- Mute audio of playing video

Up <-- With seek bar focused, increase volume 5%
Down <-- With seek bar focused, decrease volume 5%

f <-- Toggle full-screen of playing video

c <-- Toggle captions of playing video

Shift+N <-- Move to next video in playlist or next suggested video if not in playlist
Shift+P <-- Move to previous video in playlist or previous suggested video if not in playlist
