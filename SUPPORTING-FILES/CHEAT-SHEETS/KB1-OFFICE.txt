Office 365 Shortcuts
====================

The HYPER key is Control+Alt+Shift+Windows keys, all pressed simlutaneously.

AHK DEFINED
-----------
HYPER+Q <-- Open Office Hub

OFFICE PRE-DEFINED
------------------
HYPER   <-- Open Office Hub (Disabled by Registry Edit - see link below)
HYPER+D <-- Open your OneDrive in Explorer
HYPER+L <-- Open LinkedIn in browser
HYPER+N <-- Open OneNote
HYPER+O <-- Open Outlook
HYPER+P <-- Open PowerPoint
HYPER+T <-- Open Microsoft Teams
HYPER+W <-- Open Word
HYPER+X <-- Open Excel
HYPER+Y <-- Open Yammer
HYPER+Space <-- Open Emoji Menu
